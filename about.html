How Kidpreneur works

1

Kickoff workshops — pick a problem space, learn rapid prototyping, form teams.

2

Mentor sprints — weekly critiques with engineers, designers and founders.

3

Build & iterate — test with users, ship improvements, prep the demo.

4

Showcase — present to peers, teachers and partners; top teams get support.
